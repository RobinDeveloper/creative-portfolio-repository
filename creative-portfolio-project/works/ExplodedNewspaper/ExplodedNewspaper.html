Description

For this project we were working with routines. Doing something everyday. I decided to start with pixel sorting images. These images started as something random until I came over the idea to make newspaper frontpages each day. Requesting the top news stories of that day, exploding and colour changing the images, and destroying the headline to make something new and interesting. beauty in random repetition.
