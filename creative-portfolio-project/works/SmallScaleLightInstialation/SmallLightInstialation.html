Description

For this project we were tasked to make something with light, I decided to make a small scale model of a potentially huge audio visual shadowplay. The idea is that the metal ring would be a concrete curve with DMX lights built in and a place for a big glass ball with water transforming the space around it in a strobe infused light party.
