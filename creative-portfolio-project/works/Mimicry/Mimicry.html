Description

The idea for this class was Technology in nature, and I got very interested in butterflies and their wings. Wanting to use wing patters for data storage. NOT ENCRYPTION I wanted the data to be readable from the wings like a qr code. I made a proof of concept where using a genetic algorithm to breed butterflies they would form their wing patters closer and closer to the desired patter. What i'd like to do next is actually have it be based in science more and make the patters more natural looking or butterflies.
