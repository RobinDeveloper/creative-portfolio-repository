Description

For this assignment we were tasked to create a Rube Goldberg machine, which would get data do something with it and send other data to the next person. I started the chain with a venus fly trap that would get edged until it finally closed. Randomly every 29 to 40 seconds a servo would touch the hairs inside the flytrap recording the electrical output of the plant. When the plant closes because of too many stumulations to quickly the electrical system would catch that and send my data to the next person.
