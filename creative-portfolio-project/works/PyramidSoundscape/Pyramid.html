Description

For this assignment we were tasked to create a robot that would make noise, I made a self-driving robot that drove over ribbed cardboard. The sound it created along with the robots of my classmates we all made into our own soundscape and then made visualisers for them. I made this pyramid space. For the expo I removed everything except for the black hole and showed that as a hologram in the exhibition space.
