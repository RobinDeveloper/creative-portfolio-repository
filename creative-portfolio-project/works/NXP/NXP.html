Description

The promise of automation is that is would significanlty reduce car crashes. To investigate and challenge this idea, we get confronted with the amount of car accidents that happend in The Netherlands.

The instalation runs LIVE and tracks the internet for recent car crashes. The left engine is tied to general announced crashes that involve trauma, and the right one is tied to fatal crashes.

To retrieve this data, I request accident information from multiple API's every 5 minutes, when it recieves an confirmation of a car crash the motors will activate and aggresivly hit the hanging doors.
