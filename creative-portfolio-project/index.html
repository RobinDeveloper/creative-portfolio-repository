About me

I am Robin Opheij, someone who is very interested in graphics programming, softwaregore, games, and interactive art I make entertaining works fully designed for the observer to enjoy, but also works that ask critical questions about its subject.

In my works there are elements of punk, sciences, ethics, morals, machine learning, brutalism, and minimalism

I have become interested in biology, genetics, dna, bio-art, and compression. over the past year
